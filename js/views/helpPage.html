Why Parakh?

Feedbacks can make or break careers. Hence right feedback and timeliness in giving feedback are very important. Parakh helps you do that all year long without the need to wait for quarter or year ending.

If someone does good appreciate them by giving +1 right that moment, if someone is making a repeated mistake, help them by pointing it out with a-

1. Makes sense, right?

What can you do?

  • You can rate your team members and colleagues +1 if you feel that they have done an exceptional job or if your colleague has helped you do your job better.
  • You can request your team lead or manager to give a positive rating as well if you have shown good work or accomplished something outside your work area.
  • Keep your profile up to date on Parakh and give your colleagues a chance to know more about you and connect with you. You can also see your colleagues’ profile.
  • Also, see the top performers within the ICC team and see where you stand.

What it is not?

Parakh is only to track the ups and downs in your work every day. It is in no way related to the Quarterly or Annual feedback process followed by the organization.

Why Parakh?

As a lead you tend to forget the daily good or the bad that team members have done in the year. And it’s tough to honestly rate them at the end of the year. Parakh helps solve that problem as you can continue to rate the team members all year long, every single day as they do excellent work. Later on you can cite incidences when the TMs did excellent during the year and where the lacked, helping them to grow.

What can you do?

  • You can always rate your team members +1 if you feel that they have done an exceptional job or if your colleague has helped you do your job better.
  • You can also give your team member a -1 rating if you feel that the member went down the track for some time and showed low performance.
  • You can request your lead or manager to give a positive rating as well if you have shown good work or work outside your work area.
  • You can approve or decline the requests you have received from your team members by adding your comments if you wish too.
  • As a lead you can give feedback to your team members to provide them an insight to their performance, as well as advice to solve any problem.
  • Keep your profile up to date on Parakh and give your colleagues a chance to know more about you. You can also see your team members’ profile and keep a track on their performance.
Also, see the top performers within the ICC team and know where you stand.

What it is not?

Parakh is only to track the ups and downs in your work every day. It is in no way related to the Quarterly or Annual feedback process followed by the organization.
